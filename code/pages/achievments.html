In June 2012 Hope4Havering was awarded Silver in the Metropolitan Police Problem Orientated Policing Awards, in partnership with the Safer London Transport police.

In July 2012, we also were put forward for the European Goldstein Awards and came 7th in Europe. This led to Kim Merry (CEO) and Luke Spicer (Night shelter Co-ordinator) presenting along with Police Sergeant Darren Hepple in Rhode Island in front of Herman Goldstein and an international police audience.
